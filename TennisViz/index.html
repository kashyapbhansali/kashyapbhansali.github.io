Aggressive Play increases Opponent Errors!
The Scatter-Bubble Plot demonstrates a pattern of Increased Opponent Errors with an increase in player's Aggressive Play.

--- Two Interesting observations ---
  1. Andy Roddick has consistent Fast Serve speed, hence increased aces in his games and he forced more errors on his opponents with increased aggressive play.
  2. Rafael Nadal has varying Fast Serve speeds but has one of the highest Aggressive margin and forced error rates than anyone else.
